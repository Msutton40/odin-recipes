Peach Lemonade

Refreshing Peach Lemonade

This Fun and Fruity is as refreshing as it sounds. It's the perfect drink during the summer time serving cool and refresh. Serve it with whatever and you will be satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 peach syrup
  • 3 cups of water
  • 1/3 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup of sugar

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a pitcher
  2. refrigerate for 10 mins
  3. Garnish with mint and sliced peaches
