Oatmeal Cookies

Soft Oatmeal Cookies

This recipe for Oatmeal cookies is the best tasting.Their soft and chewy to the taste. Just adding a dash of cinnamon and some oats really changed the lovely flavor

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter(softened)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/4 baking powder
  • 1/4 baking soda
  • 1/4 salt
  • 1/4 Cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup raw oats
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1/2 tbs Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 250 Degrees
  2. Mix the sugars with the softened butter until completely combined. Add the vanilla extract and the egg, then mix until fully combined.
  3. Add the dry ingredients and start folding them in until all dry ingredients are fully incorporated. Fold the oats into the dough until fully incorporated.
  4. Divide the dough into little circles and place them on baking sheet and cook for 7-8 mins
